Sandwich ingredients:
  • 2 slices of bread
  • mayo

How to make a sandwich
  1. lay down 1 slice of bread
  2. put mayo on the slice of bread
  3. put the lettuce on the mayo
  4. put the tomato on the lettuce
  5. put the ham on the tomato
  6. put the cheese on the ham
  7. put the other slice of bread on top
